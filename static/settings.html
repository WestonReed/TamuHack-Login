How does this work?

Metamask is browser extension and complete wallet for the Ethereum cryptocurrency. Ethereum wallets use public-key encryption technology to identity themselves for sending and receiving payments. Metamask lets you use this technology to uniquely identify yourself to websites, like this one, without a password. To prove that you have control of the wallet your browser claims to, upon login, Metamask will ask you to cryptographically sign a special phrase we generate before every login attempt. Once confirmed, your browser is provided a cookie to access our site and your personal information for the duration of the session.
